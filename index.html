Platform Capabilities

Built for Serious HEOR Research

Purpose-built tools that meet the methodological standards expected by payers, HTA bodies, and peer-reviewed journals — without the manual overhead.

Cost-Effectiveness Analysis

Build Markov cohort and microsimulation models with automated cycle calculations, transition probability estimation, and probabilistic sensitivity analysis (PSA).

Budget Impact Modeling

Project real-world budget impact for payers and health systems with epidemiology-driven prevalence estimates, market uptake curves, and scenario analysis.

PRO & Utility Mapping

Map patient-reported outcomes (EQ-5D, SF-6D, PROMIS) to utility values and automate HRQoL analysis pipelines for clinical trial data.

AI-Assisted Study Design

Our AI co-pilot guides parameter selection, flags methodological inconsistencies, and suggests literature-backed assumptions aligned with ISPOR and NICE guidelines.

Collaborative Workspaces

Invite co-authors, biostatisticians, and medical writers. Role-based access ensures each team member sees only what they need — with full audit trails.

Publication-Ready Reports

Auto-generate structured reports with CHEERS 2022-aligned reporting, exportable model code, and one-click submission packages for HTA dossiers.

Workflow

From Data to Decision in 3 Steps

Designed to match how HEOR teams actually work — not force you into a rigid workflow.

1

Upload & Configure

Import your clinical trial data, RWE datasets, or cost data. BARD validates structure, flags missing values, and auto-suggests model parameters from your inputs.

2

Run & Validate

Execute your economic model with built-in PSA, DSA, and scenario analyses. The AI engine cross-checks outputs against known HEOR benchmarks and highlights anomalies.

3

Export & Submit

Generate CHEERS-compliant reports, downloadable model code (R/Excel), and formatted outputs ready for HTA submissions, ISPOR posters, or peer review.
